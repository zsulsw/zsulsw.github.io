PhD Supervision

Dr. Liu supervises a cohort of doctoral students working on state‑of‑the‑art topics in structural engineering and construction.

  • OUYANG Weihang (2021 – Present) – Second‑order analysis of pile‑supported structures considering soil–structure interaction.
